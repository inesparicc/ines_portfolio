Bloom Habit Coach — nurturing gentle progress

Role: Product Designer Timeline: 6 weeks Team: 3 designers, 1 developer

Designed a habit-forming mobile experience that pairs compassionate nudges with mood insights so that young professionals can sustain wellness rituals without guilt.

Opportunity

User research revealed that most apps made participants feel judged when they broke a streak. We reframed success around reflection and self-kindness, creating a product vision grounded in emotional wellbeing.

Highlights

  • Established a soft visual language using dusty pinks, lavender gradients and rounded components to convey warmth.
  • Introduced “check-in stories” that let users jot down feelings, unlocking adaptive suggestions for the next day.
  • Designed a progress garden dashboard with growth rings and subtle motion to celebrate small wins.
  • Partnered with engineering to hand off a Figma design system covering states, motion specs and accessibility tokens.

Impact

After beta testing with twelve participants, daily engagement increased by 28% and qualitative feedback highlighted how the voice felt “supportive, not preachy.” The work informed Bloom Studio’s broader wellbeing product roadmap.
