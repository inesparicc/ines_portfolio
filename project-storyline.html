Storyline Library — designing for quiet delight

Role: UI Designer & Design System Lead Timeline: 8 weeks Tools: Figma, After Effects

Reimagined a digital library platform with inclusive typography, story-first navigation and soft interactions that invite readers to linger.

Context

The existing product struggled with accessibility and a dated brand. Readers felt rushed by transactional UI and couldn’t easily discover new authors. We set out to craft a system that honoured quiet reading rituals.

Design system

  • Developed a modular component library with responsive card grids, shelf layouts and motion tokens.
  • Created a typographic scale blending Playfair Display and modern sans-serifs for readability across devices.
  • Delivered contrast-safe palettes anchored in dusty rose, dawn beige and midnight ink.
  • Documented tone of voice guidelines and empty states that celebrate curiosity instead of urgency.

Results

Post-launch analytics showed a 35% increase in time spent exploring collections and a 22% lift in saved titles. The design system now powers future campaigns and seasonal storytelling experiments.
